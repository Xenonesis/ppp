Management Contract

Definition & Key Features

An agreement where a private entity is responsible for managing and operating a publicly-owned asset or service

Medium-term (3-10 years) Public ownership maintained Performance-based payment
Operational control transferred to private entity
Performance incentives for efficiency gains
Limited investment responsibility by private sector

Real-World Examples

Water Utilities

Manila Water Company (Philippines) achieved 95% service coverage and 24/7 water supply through management contract

Public Transport

London Underground's management contract resulted in 30% reduction in delays and 20% cost savings

Healthcare

UK's NHS hospital management contracts improved patient satisfaction by 40% and reduced waiting times

Ports

Port of Rotterdam management contract increased container throughput by 25% while reducing costs
