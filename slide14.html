BTO (Build-Transfer-Operate)

Definition & Key Features

A PPP model where a private entity builds an infrastructure facility, transfers ownership to the public sector upon completion, and then operates it under a concession agreement

Long-term (20-30 years) Public ownership Private operation
Immediate transfer of ownership after construction
Private operation under long-term agreement
Public sector retains asset ownership

BTO Lifecycle

BUILD
Private entity constructs the facility
TRANSFER
Ownership transferred to public sector
OPERATE
Private entity operates under concession
