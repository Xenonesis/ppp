Introduction to Public-Private Partnership

What is PPP?

A long-term contractual arrangement between public and private sectors to deliver public infrastructure/services with shared risks, resources & expertise

Long-term Partnership

20-30 year contracts with shared objectives

Risk Allocation

Optimal distribution between public & private entities

Private Financing

Leverages private capital for public infrastructure

Performance-based

Payments linked to measurable outcomes
